Routine



      How to Use

      This site automatically loads a task list based on your local time (AM or PM).

      You can use one NFC tag or link to load your Morning or Evening routine, depending on when you tap it. You can copy the URL or click the 'copy link' button in the gear menu

      All your info is saved locally on this device only. If you clear your cache or switch devices, your tasks and history will be lost.

      If you plan to switch devices and want to keep lists and data, please export your data using the gear menu and re-upload the JSON to restore your list and tracker.
